Top 5 Best Restaurants In Dubai You Need To Try RIGHT AWAY

BY ALEX MERCER · PUBLISHED: JAN 10, 2022 · MODIFIED: FEB 4, 2022

Dubai has a vibrant food scene, and sometimes the options thrown at you can be overwhelming. So, if you’re planning to dine outside, you might be confused on what to pick. But fret not! We’ve scouted a list of restaurants that have a certified 4.9 rating from Zomato.

Here are the 10 best-rated restaurants in Dubai for you to try out now! Not only are they spot on with their flavours, but they are also diverse and cater to a wide audience. So get ready for the binge!

1. Carnival By Tresind

Carnival by Tresind boasts a punked-up ambiance and offers a flavorful collection of modern Indian cuisine with a theme-based seasonal menu. The restaurant serves conventional Indian food with a modern twist. Besides, there’s also a story behind each dish, making the experience even more interesting. Playful in its creation and tasteful to the palette, a must-have culinary experience.

Burj Daman, DIFC, www.carnivalbytresind.com (052 242 4262).

2. Socialicious

Chill down with your friends at Socialicious and explore lesser traveled flavors of Asia. On offer are Vietnamese Baos as well as the Burmese Khao Suey. Want to up your fusion scale? Try our their Edamame Falafel and catch up with your social life here.

Burj Daman, DIFC, www.carnivalbytresind.com (052 242 4262).

3. Texas De Brazil

This Brazilian steakhouse serves its Churascaro the bold way- the Texas way. Prepared over an open flame, this eatery features an all you can eat meat spread. You don’t need a green card to explore the Texas hospitality with this one!.

Mall Of Emirates, Barsha, www.taxasdebrazil.com (052 242 4262).

4. Marina Social

With new, innovative and utterly delicious dishes on the menu, one of Dubai Marina’s best restaurants has returned to the top of this category. Marina Social last picked up this award in 2017, and has regained its title thanks to glorious plates that balance creativity with a just-can’t-stop-eating-it factor. On the menu you’ll find everything from 15-hour barbecue short-rib and beef wellington to lobster linguine, while service is first-class. All that combined with views across the Marina from the terrace, and you’ve got the makings of a memorable meal out.

InterContinental Dubai Marina, www.marinasocialdubai.com (04 446 6664).

5. LPM Restaurant & Bar

No stranger to the awards podium, LPM Restaurant & Bar is a stalwart on our list of best French restaurants in Dubai, and a multiple winner of this title – and that’s due to its unwavering consistency. From a menu crammed with classics, there isn’t a dish that falls short of excellent. Don’t miss the perfectly pink lamb cutlets, the rich, indulgent dauphinoise potatoes and the beautiful, juicy prawns in olive oil. There’s a buzzing vibe and it’s a top spot whether a date night or business lunch.

Gate Village 8, DIFC, www.lpmlondon.co.uk/dubai (04 439 0505).
